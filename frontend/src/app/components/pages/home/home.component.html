About Us


Doryoku (努⼒) is a Japanese word that means "effort" or "endeavor". It is a concept deeply ingrained in Japanese culture and is often associated with the idea of "Ganbaru", which roughly translates to "doing one's best" or "persevering". We have taken from Japanese culture for there is a strong emphasis on the value of hard work and persistence and Doryoku Holdings is seen as an important part of achieving success in various aspects of life. The idea is that one can overcome obstacles and achieve their desired outcomes through consistent effort and dedication. We value the workplace, where employees are expected to work diligently and take on challenges with a positive attitude. This became one of the core reasons for the birth of Doryoku Holdings. Making "Doryoku" a fundamental concept in our holding company as a culture that reflects the importance placed on hard work, perseverance and dedication as keys to achieving success.
